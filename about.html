About Me

Systems, automation, and a bias toward outcomes

I'm Gabriel Rodriguez, a GoHighLevel Specialist and workflow automation consultant working with service-based businesses across HVAC, dental, and fitness industries.

My Story

From single builds to full digital systems

I started by building GoHighLevel websites for local businesses that needed more than a static page — they needed a system that captured leads, followed up automatically, and kept a pipeline moving without constant manual input.

That work grew into full CRM builds, multi-step funnels, and automation sequences for HVAC companies, dental clinics, and fitness businesses, supported by AI tools like Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini to move faster without cutting corners on quality or SOP adherence.
